i.MX6Q (Cortex-A9)
==================

EPC/PPC-A9 series of industrial PCs equipped with the powerful quad-core Arm Cortex-A9 based **CS-SOM-iMX6Q** SoM operating at 1.2 Hz delivers a high integration level and power-efficient 
processing capabilities, making it the perfect solution for the broadest range of highly demanding applications.
